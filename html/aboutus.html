About us
Athletica aims to make fitness a family affair by providing sports and coaching to kids and the best gymming experience to adults.We desire to bring you the best possible training experience irrespective of your age or current fitness level. We have different trainers for your different needs who are categorized based on their expertise in their specific domains. We also provide coaching and non-coaching memberships for various sports like badminton, table tennis, swimming, and squash. We have international standard badminton courts, 6 table tennis courts and a 30 meters track on the gym floor. Furthermore, our gym is equipped with the heaviest dumbbells in the city and is the only gym in the city that is hammer strength certified.
